Advertising and AdMob

Our game uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use:

  • Advertising ID: Used for personalized advertising
  • Device Information: Model, OS version, and device type
  • IP Address: For approximate location targeting
  • Ad Interactions: Clicks, impressions, and conversion data

Personalized vs Non-Personalized Ads

Users can opt-out of personalized advertising through their device settings: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

Google's Data Collection

As a publisher using AdMob, we must disclose that Google collects data according to their own privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
