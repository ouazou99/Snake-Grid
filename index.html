Transparency Notice: This privacy policy explains how our Snake Game handles user information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

Our Snake Game is designed with privacy as a core principle. We want to be completely transparent about what we do not collect:

Personal Information

  • No names, email addresses, or contact information
  • No birth dates or age information
  • No location data or GPS information
  • No phone numbers or device identifiers
  • No social media accounts or profiles

Device Information

  • No camera or microphone access
  • No contact list access
  • No photo or media library access
  • No call history or SMS data
  • No device storage access (except for local game data)

Sensitive Data

  • No health or fitness data

  • No authentication credentials
  • No browsing history
  • No purchase history

2. Data Collection & Usage

Local Game Data (Device Storage Only)

The game stores the following information locally on your device:

  • High Scores: Your personal best scores
  • Game Preferences: Difficulty settings and control options
  • Game Progress: Current game state when paused

This data remains exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or third parties.

No Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or trade any user information with third parties because we don't collect any information to share.

3. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Important: Our game is suitable for users of all ages, including children under 13.

Our Commitment to Child Safety

  • No personal information collection from any users
  • No in-app purchases or advertisements
  • No social features or user interaction
  • No external links within the game
  • No data retention beyond local device storage

Parental Controls

Since we don't collect any data, parental concerns about data privacy are eliminated. The game operates entirely offline and all data stays on the device.

4. Permissions

5. Third-Party Services

Our game does not integrate with any third-party services that would collect user data:

While we don't collect or store user data on our servers, we ensure that local device storage is handled securely through standard Android security measures.
